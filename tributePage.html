About Rock Lee

The Man, The Myth, The Legend - As a shinobi of the Lee clan from Konohagakure, Rock Lee was one of the more interesting characters to be introduced within the Naruto world. We are first shown his skills when he decides to challenge Sasuke to a fight before the chunin exam. It is then that his prowess in taijutsu is first displayed. As the story progressed forwarded, it is shown that Rock Lee's refined taijutsu skill is made to compensate for his inability to perform genjutsu or ninjutsu. As one of the few shinobi in the Hidden Leaf Village that can't perform genjutsu or ninjutsu, Lee is mocked as he goes through schooling. Despite this, Lee becomes determined to prove that he can be a splendid shinobi. His pure determination and work ethic become his defining characteristics throughout the anime.

Top 5 Rock Lee Episodes

1) Lee vs. Gaara - The episode that Lee and Gaara fight is easily one of his most iconic. This is where we get to see what this shinobi is truly capable of. Despite his inability to perform ninjutsu or genjutsu, Lee heads into the battle head first and without hesitation. Obviously, the coolest moment of this fight is when Lee finally takes off his ankle weights. As he does this, what seemed like normal weights, prove to be amazingly heavy as they cause a small explosion as they hit the ground. With the weights off, Lee's speed increases exponentially. Through sheer physical ability, Lee is able to land a blow on Gaara. This scene is made more amazing considering that Gaara is know to have the unbreakable defense of Shukaku the one-tailed beast within him.

Lee landing a hit on Gaara (Episode 48)

2) First Appearance - Rock Lee is first introduced to Team Kakashi right before the Chunin exams begin. This is an important point in shinobi life, as young genin are attempting to move up in the ranks and become Chunin ranked ninja. Lee's humorous character design is shown as he professes his love for Sakura. This leads him to trying to display his taijutsu dominance by challenging Sasuke. Up to this point, Sasuke is depicted as a formidable shinobi of the Uchiha Clan possessing the sharingan eyes. With these eyes, Sasuke's fighting ability is heightened and can even imitate some opponent's jutsu. This is where we learn that Rock Lee fights purely based on taijutsu. In the fight Lee gains the upperhand until he is quickly stopped by Guy Sensei.

Lee fighting Sasuke (Episode 22)

3) Drunken Fist Prodigy - Lee's fight with Kimimaro is probably one of the most important and funniest fights that he has throughout Naruto. This fight is incredibly crucial as it is part of one of the major arcs in the show. In this arc, Naruto, Shikamaru and others are chasing after Orochimaru's Sound Four. The reason that this is so important is because they are attempting to kidnap Sasuke and bring him to Orochimaru. Later we find out that Orochimaru's intention is to get rid of his dying body and take control of Sasuke's. During the pursuit, members of Naruto's party are split off to take on the Sound Four members in individual battles. Because the Sound Four members were taking to long to return with Sasuke, Kimimaro decides to step in and fight. In order to allow Naruto to continue after Sasuke, Lee decides to fight as well. Kimimaro is shown to be a frightening opponent as he possesses a rare kekkei genkai that gives him full control of his bones that he he can create, make dense, and use as weapons. An important thing to note, is that at this point in the show, Lee is still recovering from his battle from Gaara where it was thought that he would never fight again. This is why we are fortunately able to see Lee's true skill, the drunken fist. After taking his "medicine" Lee's fighting becomes heightened, but also erratic. This hilarious fighting style proves to be one of the strongest in the show in terms of taijutsu and even becomes a fierce challenge for Kimimaro.

Lee fights Kimimaro (Episode 126)

4) Student vs. Master - During the third great beast arc we finally see a serious battle between Guy and Lee. These two characters are easily the best taijutsu fighters in the entire show. They are even able to open the inner gates to heighten their already amazing abilities. In this episode Lee and Guy are trapped in a makeshift temple made up of wood from dummy fighting partners that are under the control of an enemy's jutsu. As the two fight their way up the tower, they must continously fight these wooden dummies. Eventually, Guy and Lee unknowingly start to fight each other as the enemy's jutsu makes them look like wooden dummies to each other. Guy and Lee become amazed by the dummy that they are fighting as they realize its awesome taijutsu skill. The pair eventually realize that they are fighting each other and decide that going all out is the only solution. In amazing display of their talents, their sheer speed and force in their attacks is too much for the jutsu to handle and they are eventually freed.

Lee fighting Guy during the 3rd Great Beast Arc (Episode 196)

5) Challenger Wanted - In one of the more light-hearted episodes, Lee builds a dojo so that he can go to other dojos and challenge the students to taijutsu fights. The purpose for this is to get stronger and prove that he is the best taijutsu fighter around. Proud of his student, Guy decides to disguise himself and challenge Lee in his dojo. Unfortunately, Guy gets sent off on a mission before he can. At the same time, a thief named Nanafushi shows up disguise as Guy and ends up getting forced to fight Lee in his dojo. Eventually, the thief gets destroyed by Lee's taijutsu and he is turned in to Tsunade.
